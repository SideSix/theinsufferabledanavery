About Dan

I received a B.A., Cum Laude, from Macalester College, in Saint Paul, Minnesota and a M.F.A. in Fiction Writing from The University of California, Irvine. I'm a poet who hand-crafts web sites.

Here's what I'm most proud of: when my future mother-in-law asked Norm to tell her something about me he said, "I've known Dan for over twenty years and he's never once lied to me."
